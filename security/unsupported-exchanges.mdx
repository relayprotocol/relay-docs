Below is a list of third-party crypto exchanges that are not supported by Relay. The list is not exhaustive. Uneven Labs reserves the right to update this list as may be appropriate from time to time at its sole discretion.

- @CryptoBot
- @wallet
- Bit Pin
- BitcoinVN
- Bitnest
- ChangeHero
- ChangeNOW
- CoinEx
- Crypto HK
- Cryptomus
- FixedFloat
- Heleket
- HTX (Huobi Global)
- Kraken Market
- N.Exchange
- Sideshift.ai
- Tornado.cash
- Wallex.ir
- Exmo.me

### What this means for you

- **Withdrawals from a blocked exchange:** Do not withdraw funds from a blocked exchange directly to a Relay deposit address. The deposit will be blocked and the transaction cannot be completed.
- **Sending to a blocked exchange:** Quotes where the recipient address is associated with a blocked exchange will be rejected with a `BLOCKED_WALLET_ADDRESS` error.

### Our policy

Blocking decisions are based on real-time screening from professional risk and sanctions databases, combined with Relay's internal blocklist. Relay does not make case-by-case exceptions for sanctioned entities. For details on how screening works, see [Compliance](/security/compliance).

For additional information, contact us via [Support](/resources/support).
