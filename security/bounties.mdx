---
title: 'Bounties'
description: 'Learn how Relay rewards developers who find & report vulnerabilities.'
---

Relay is committed to maintaining the highest security standards for its protocol and smart contracts.
The bug bounty program incentivizes those who help identify vulnerabilities in the Relay Protocol.
The bounty program aims to incentivize ethical hackers to discover and report vulnerabilities in Relay's contracts and protocol.

Rewards are dependent on the severity of the bug. Relay always provides monetary rewards for bugs that fall under these categories:

- Extract value from the solver
- Exploiting Relay's smart contracts in an unintended way that results in a loss of funds

To be eligible for the monetary rewards, this bug must be investigated and confirmed by the Relay team.
Monetary reward amounts are 10% of the value at risk, with a maximum reward capped at $100,000 USD. "Value at risk" is defined as the total value that could be extracted within a 10 minute period. This is because the protocol has automatic circuit breakers and alarms that would prevent losses that take longer to extract.

If you suspect you've encountered a bug, please reach out directly by email: [support@relay.link](emailto:support@relay.link).
The Relay team reviews all reports closely.
Expect a response within 1 to 4 days regarding the next steps.
Most responses are provided within 24 hours, barring holidays and weekends.
