title: 'Deposit Address'
description: 'Learn how to use Relay with a deposit address for instant swapping.'

Relay supports instantly and cheaply swaping from a variety of EVM and non-EVM chain to any token. We are now launching deposit addresseds to allow users to swap with connecitng a wallet. 

Using a deposit address is as simple as sending funds to a designated adressed on any support chain and receiving funds on a different chain
