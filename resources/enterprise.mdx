## What is an Enterprise Partner?

An Enterprise partner is a team that meets certain criteria outlined below. These teams are granted access to exclusive features and our team will work with them to craft a custom contract to support their use case.

After becoming an Enterprise Partner you'll get access to the following:
- Fee Sponsorship
- Fast Fills
- Custom SLAs
- Priority Support
- Increased Rate Limits
- Revenue Share

## How to Become an Enterprise Partner

To become an enterprise partner, you must meet the following criteria:

- You must be expecting or currently processing $500M+ in daily volume.
- You must apply using this [form](https://forms.gle/XNeELYavjwoJwPEf7). Our team will review your application and reach out to you with next steps if considered.
