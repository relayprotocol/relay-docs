## Scoring Criteria

Use this framework to calculate **Priority Score** (0-100):

### Volume Score (40 points max)
- $10M+ daily: 40 points
- $5M-$10M daily: 35 points
- $2M-$5M daily: 30 points
- $1M-$2M daily: 25 points
- $500K-$1M daily: 20 points
- Under $500K daily: 10 points

### Strategic Fit (25 points max)
- High-profile brand/ecosystem impact: 25 points
- Strong product-market fit: 20 points
- Moderate strategic value: 15 points
- Limited strategic value: 10 points

### Revenue Potential (20 points max)
- Revenue share opportunity: 20 points
- High fee volume expected: 15 points
- Moderate fee volume: 10 points
- Low fee volume: 5 points

### Technical Readiness (15 points max)
- Ready to integrate immediately: 15 points
- Integration within 1 month: 12 points
- Integration within 3 months: 8 points
- Integration timeline unclear: 5 points

## Status Categories

**🟢 Active Lead** - In conversation, moving forward
**🟡 Qualified** - Meets criteria, pending outreach
**🔵 Nurture** - Not ready yet, follow up later
**🟠 Pending Review** - New submission, needs evaluation
**⚪ Disqualified** - Does not meet partnership criteria
**✅ Closed-Won** - Partnership agreement signed

## Pipeline Stages

### Stage 1: Initial Review (24-48 hours)
- Review form submission
- Calculate priority score
- Assign status
- Schedule initial call if qualified

### Stage 2: Discovery Call
- Validate volume claims
- Understand use case and requirements
- Assess technical capabilities
- Identify key decision makers

### Stage 3: Technical Assessment
- Review integration requirements
- Discuss SLA needs
- Estimate timeline and resources
- Propose custom contract terms

### Stage 4: Proposal & Negotiation
- Send partnership proposal
- Negotiate terms (fees, revenue share, SLAs)
- Legal review
- Contract finalization

### Stage 5: Onboarding
- Technical integration support
- Assign dedicated account manager
- Set up monitoring and reporting
- Launch partnership

## Notes & Best Practices

- Always respond to qualified leads within 24 hours
- For $2M+ daily volume, escalate to senior leadership immediately
- Keep detailed notes in CRM/tracking system
- Schedule regular check-ins with active leads
- Maintain relationship with nurture leads through quarterly touchpoints
