Relay is designed to make transacting across chains as fast, cheap, and reliable as possible. There are several core use cases of Relay that make it a great choice for both self-custodial wallets and wallet/account infrastructure providers.

## How To Use Relay

**[Onboarding](/use-cases/bridging)** - Using Relay, wallets and wallet providers can allow users to instantly onboard crypto from any asset on any chain. This means you can source users from all major networks, and get them into your wallet in two seconds.

**[In Wallet Swaps & Bridging](/use-cases/cross-chain-swaps)** - Relay supports bridging, same-chain swaps, and crosschain swaps through a unified [Quote API](/references/api/get-quote-v2). This allows wallets to embed bridging and swaps directly into the wallet experience. Relay couples instant crosschain routing with meta-aggregation of the best onchain swap providers to give your users great prices, instant exchange across chains, and quick quotes.

**[Chain Abstraction](/use-cases/calling)** - Relay is designed to enable multichain transaction execution, also known as _multichain call execution_. That means that your users can pay for any transactions on any chain with assets on any other. In the context of wallets this is particularly useful for chain abstraction use cases and products. Relay has broad support for smart wallet types, including 4337 & 7702. Learn more about [Smart Wallet Support](/references/api/api_guides/smart_accounts/smart-accounts).

## Relay Benefits

**Chain Support** - Relay supports [85+ chains](/references/api/api_resources/supported-chains), offering coverage across all major EVM networks, alt L1s, SVMs and many appchains. Relay is designed to add chains on Day 1, and the team works closely with leading networks to ensure coverage. If there is a network you are hoping Relay will support, please reach out!

**Industry Leading Quote & Fill Times** - For bridging, swaps, and crosschain executions, Relay is industry leading in quote and fill times. The infrastructure is optimized to reduce latency across the user experience, meaning fast quotes and fast fills. The p50 fill time across chains is less than 3 seconds across all networks.

**SLAs & Customer Support** - Relay is your partner in delivering a world-class multichain experience. The Relay team is happy to offer SLAs on quote and fill speed, network uptime, and more.

**App Fees** - Relay's comprehensive approach to [App Fees](/features/app-fees) lets you easily earn when using the protocol. Additionally, the fee system is designed for you to automatically collect fees in stables (USDC).

## Integrating Relay

Integrating Relay is as simple as calling the [Quote API](/references/api/get-quote-v2). Whether you are integrating Relay for Bridging, Swaps, or Call Execution, this unified interface is the primary way to access the protocol. Please feel free to reach out with any questions, or start with the [Quickstart Guide](/references/api/quickstart).
